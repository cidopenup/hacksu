About Our Technology

Advanced solutions for environmental monitoring and protection

Satellite Monitoring

Our system utilizes high-resolution satellite imagery with 1-meter precision to track forest changes in real-time. We process multi-spectral data from multiple satellites including Landsat-8 and Sentinel-2 to detect even subtle changes in vegetation cover.

  • Real-time monitoring with 12-hour update frequency
  • Multi-spectral analysis using NIR and SWIR bands
  • Historical data comparison spanning 10 years
  • Cloud-penetrating radar technology for all-weather monitoring

AI Analysis

Powered by state-of-the-art deep learning models trained on millions of satellite images, our AI can identify and classify various types of environmental changes with over 95% accuracy.

  • Advanced CNN architecture for pattern recognition
  • Automated change detection algorithms
  • Machine learning-based risk prediction
  • Real-time anomaly detection system

Risk Assessment

Our comprehensive risk assessment system combines multiple data sources to provide accurate predictions and impact analysis of environmental changes.

  • Biodiversity impact scoring system
  • Carbon footprint calculation and tracking
  • Water resource monitoring and quality analysis
  • Predictive modeling for future risk scenarios

Cloud Processing

Distributed cloud computing infrastructure processes over 500TB of satellite data daily, enabling real-time analysis and rapid response capabilities.

Mobile Alerts

Instant notifications and alerts through our mobile app keep stakeholders informed about critical environmental changes and potential risks.

Data Integration

Seamless integration with multiple data sources including weather stations, ground sensors, and historical databases for comprehensive analysis.

Verified Accuracy

Our system maintains 95%+ accuracy in deforestation detection, validated through extensive ground-truth data and expert verification.
